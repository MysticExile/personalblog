About

Welcome on my personal site! My name is Ethan Baboeram. I am 18 years old, and I was born and raised in The Netherlands. I made this site to log my projects and creations so please have a look around!

Originally this site was meant to be a school project. I expanded on that project to make it a little more personal.
I am a creative being at heart who loves to sketch, hum and think of ideas. Most of them end up in the corner of my brain. But in the rare event I do go through with an idea, I like to make it grand. I mainly dabble in programming, electrical engineering, video content creation and music. Because of the major I do, I am often limited to programming. So in my free time I like to focus on the other aspects of my interests. But I feel like I have talked too much already, please have a look at my projects and creations!
